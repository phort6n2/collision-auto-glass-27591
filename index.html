Frequently Asked Questions About Windshield Replacement

How long does a windshield replacement take, and can I wait while you do it?
Most windshield replacements take 60-90 minutes to complete. You're welcome to wait in our comfortable waiting area, or we can arrange to pick up and deliver your vehicle within the Cedar Mill and Beaverton area for your convenience. After installation, we recommend waiting at least one hour before driving to allow the adhesive to properly cure, especially important given Oregon's frequent rain and humidity changes.
What's included in your windshield calibration service, and why is it necessary?
Our ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) calibration service recalibrates your vehicle's safety cameras and sensors after windshield replacement. This includes forward collision warning, lane departure systems, and automatic emergency braking. Modern vehicles require this calibration to function safely - without it, these critical safety features may not work properly. We use manufacturer-approved equipment and follow OEM specifications to ensure your safety systems operate exactly as designed.
Do you work with insurance companies, and what will my out-of-pocket cost be?
Yes, we work directly with all major insurance providers and can handle the entire claims process for you. Many comprehensive policies cover windshield replacement with little to no deductible - some Oregon insurers even waive deductibles entirely for glass claims. We'll verify your coverage and provide an exact quote before any work begins. For customers paying out-of-pocket, our windshield replacements typically range from $300-$600 depending on your vehicle's make, model, and required features.
Are you licensed and what kind of warranty do you provide on windshield replacements?
We are fully licensed and insured in Oregon, and our technicians are certified in both auto glass installation and ADAS calibration. We provide a lifetime warranty on our workmanship and a manufacturer's warranty on the glass itself. This covers any leaks, installation defects, or calibration issues. Given Cedar Mill's wet climate, we're particularly careful with our seal work to prevent water intrusion, and our warranty gives you peace of mind through all of Oregon's weather conditions.
Can you come to my location in Cedar Mill, and what if it's raining?
Yes, we offer mobile service throughout Cedar Mill, Bethany, and the surrounding West Hills area. However, we cannot perform windshield installations in active rain or when surfaces are wet, as this compromises the adhesive bond and safety of the installation. If weather becomes an issue during your scheduled appointment, we'll either wait for conditions to improve or reschedule at no charge. For guaranteed same-day service regardless of weather, you can always bring your vehicle to our shop facility.
